Greenlight

How to play

  1. The passenger is the console. Tap Deal a pitch, then read the two actors and genre aloud.
  2. Together, brainstorm the movie. The driver should only listen and talk, keeping eyes on the road.
  3. Halfway through, the console gets a studio note. Read it aloud and force the pitch to pivot.
  4. When brainstorm time ends, deliver a 30-second final logline. No winner, no score - just decide if the imaginary studio greenlights it.
  5. Tap New pitch whenever the car needs a new terrible masterpiece.

Real public names are used as creative prompt ingredients only. No endorsement, affiliation, or appearance is implied.

This page runs entirely on your device. No internet needed, no cookies, no storage, no tracking, no analytics - just pure fun.
