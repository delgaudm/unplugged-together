Court Procedure

  1. The passenger is the court clerk and operates the phone. The driver plays entirely by listening and talking.
  2. Read the hot take aloud. The driver chooses Agree or Disagree; the passenger must argue the opposite.
  3. In Turnabout, the Gavel drops halfway through and both players immediately swap positions.
  4. After the arguments, issue a ruling or declare the court in chaos. There is no final winner.
  5. Save the best arguments as Case Law. They remain available on this device for future legal precedent.

Road rule: the driver keeps eyes and hands off the phone. Petty Court works fully offline. Case Law and seen cases are stored only in this browser.
