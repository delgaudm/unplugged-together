How to Play

  1. The passenger reads both choices aloud. The driver keeps eyes and hands off the phone.
  2. Everyone must choose A or B. "Both," "neither," and "I don't know" are not options.
  3. Commit at the same time, then mark whether the group agreed or split.
  4. Use the discussion prompt to defend the choice. Tap Make it harder when the answer came too easily.
  5. There are no points and no winner. A passionately overthought answer is the entire point.
