How to Play

  1. The passenger draws a card and reads it aloud. The driver only listens and talks, keeping eyes and hands off the phone.
  2. Both people answer completely honestly. An answer is not finished until the other person understands the reasoning behind it.
  3. Tap One more layer whenever an answer deserves a better follow-up.
  4. There are no points, timers, correct answers, or winners. The conversation is the entire game.
  5. Either person may say Not tonight to any card. No explanation is required.
