How to Play

  1. Each team has two players. One sees the category; the other looks away and guesses.
  2. The clue-giver may say examples only: for "Things that are hot," try "the sun, a stove, fresh coffee."
  3. Do not define the phrase, use or alter an important word printed on the card, or gesture. Treat a violation as Pass / Foul.
  4. Tap Correct or Pass / Foul to move immediately to the next card. Finish as many as possible before time expires.
  5. After every team plays, the highest total wins. A tied lead carries into another round for only those tied teams.
