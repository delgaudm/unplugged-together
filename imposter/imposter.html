Imposter

How to play

  1. Pass the phone around the table. Each player holds a thumb on the card to privately see it. Everyone sees the same secret location — except one player, who sees only that they are the imposter.
  2. Place the phone face-up in the middle. It shows the timer and the list of possible locations — the secret is one of them.
  3. Take turns asking each other questions about the place: "Would you bring grandma here?" Whoever just answered asks next (but not right back at their asker). Answer well enough to prove you know it, vaguely enough not to give it away.
  4. Anyone may stop the round and accuse. Majority points at the accused: right, and the table wins — wrong, and the imposter wins. The imposter may also interrupt at any moment to guess the location: right guess, imposter wins.
  5. If the timer runs out with no one caught, the imposter wins. Tap End round and hold to reveal the truth.

Best with 4–8 players, ages 8 and up. Play several rounds so everyone gets a chance to be the imposter — or not know whether they will be.
